Things to Do

We're so excited to share a few of our favorite places in the area with you.

Cart-Driver Pizza

2500 Larimer Street, #100
Denver, CO 80205

One of the best pizza spots in town in the trendy RiNo neighborhood. They have fresh oysters and prosecco on tap – what could be better!

Shells & Sauce

2600 E 12th Avenue
Denver, CO 80206

Shells & Sauce is a jazzy little trattoria serving classic & creative fare, plus brunch & happy hour on the roof deck.

Ratio Beerworks

2920 Larimer Street
Denver, CO 80205

Ratio is one of our favorite breweries in town, serving up an array of great beers. Take a seat on their patio and catch some rays.

Museum of Contemporary Art

1485 Delganey Street
Denver, CO 80202

The Museum of Contemporary Art, in Denver, Colorado, was founded in 1996 as the first dedicated home for contemporary art in the city of Denver.

Stowaway Kitchen

2528 Walnut Street
Denver, CO 80205

Stowaway is a cozy café offering a full espresso bar, plus globally influenced breakfast & lunch fare. A great place to grab a bite in RiNo.

Clyfford Still Museum

1250 Bannock Street
Denver, CO 80204

The Clyfford Still Museum is a minimalist showcase for the large-scale paintings & archives of the namesake Abstract Expressionist.

Denver Art Museum

100 W 14th Ave Parkway
Denver, CO 80204

The Denver Art Museum – DAM is an art museum located in the Civic Center of Denver, Colorado. The museum is one of the largest art museums between the West Coast and Chicago.

Little Man Ice Cream

2620 16th Street
Denver, CO 80211

Little Man Ice Cream is a milk-jug shaped shop scooping unique flavors of homemade ice cream & open until midnight.

Port Side

2500 Larimer Street, #103
Denver, CO 80205

Port Side is a casual, stylish coffeehouse offering house-roasted beans, espresso drinks & creative light bites.

Topo Designs

2500 Larimer Street, #102
Denver, CO 80205

This is the flagship store for Topo's high-design, colorful, USA-made outdoor gear, apparel & bags.

Denver Central Market

2669 Larimer Street
Denver, CO 80205

Meat & fish counters plus a bakery, pizzeria, bar & much more in an airy marketplace.

The Source

3500 Brighton Boulevard
Denver, CO 80216

Denver's first food hall, this bustling artisan food market located in a landmark 1880s building, features 13 vendors & a hotel. The Source is also home to one of our favorite breweries — Crooked Stave.

Our Mutual Friend Brewing Company

2810 Larimer Street
Denver, CO 80205

Award-winning Denver brewery with great offerings and a wonderful patio.

Cerebral Brewing

1477 Monroe Street
Denver, CO 80206

Cerebral Brewing is focused on crafting beer of the highest quality and consistency. All offered with a scientific approach.

Zeppelin Station

3501 Wazee Street, Suite #100
Denver, CO 80216

Food hall with eight vendors serving banh mi, fried chicken, gelato & more, plus two cocktail bars.

Curtis Park Deli

2532 Champa Street
Denver, CO 80206

A neighborhood delicatessen that prides themselves on serving Denver’s freshest sandwiches. They stack and wrap them daily, made with just-baked ciabatta, clean meats, and crisp, seasonal veggies.

Spur Coffee

95 Lincoln Street
Denver, CO 80203

Spur Coffee offers some of the best cups of joe in town. Their pastries and food items are all made from scratch daily with fresh and wholesome ingredients.

Meek Vintage

39 W 11th Avenue
Denver, CO 80204

Meek is one of our favorite places to shop in town. Meek offers modern and vintage home goods, clothing, and of course plants.

Kirkland Museum of Fine & Decorative Art

1201 Bannock Street
Denver, CO 80204

3,500+ art nouveau, art deco, Pop Art & postmodern works & Western landscapes by Vance Kirkland.

Leven Deli Co.

123 W 12th Avenue
Denver, CO 80204

With sentimental attachment to the classic American Deli, Leven is a modern take on the traditional deli model. From food, to drinks and design, Leven is built to be a new type of deli better fit for today.
